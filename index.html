Hi, I'm Meredith!

I love designing and creating applications to help fill a need.
I began my journey as a developer through the full-stack web development program at The University of Pennsylvania, and fell in love with the process of problem-solving through tech.
The story telling elements of identifying what people need, and creating an application to help address real issues is what makes working as a developer so fullfilling for me.
I love the hidden natural spaces around Philadelphia, and the wildlife who call our city home. When I'm not in front of my computer, you'll find me exploring local hiking trails, indulging my inner beer nerd at local breweries, or being a proud mom to my two beautiful house-rabbits.

Recent Projects

Collaborative Projects

The three projects below were created in collaboration with groups of colleagues from the full-stack developer course at Upenn. These projects gave us the opportunity to work synergistically on building applications, and fine-tune the process of integrating our ideas so everyone's voice was heard, as well as coordinating code from multiple developers on github.

Fridge For All

An inventory tracking application to help community fridges in Philadelphia. With this interactive app, both donors and people collecting food from the fridge can easily adjust the count of a given item.

More about the project

Happy Trails

A hike planning app, enabling you to search for area trails based on distance from your location, and desired length of your hike. Searches generate a list of trails matching input criteria.

More about the project



Shop & Chef

An application which allows users to easily create shopping lists, search for recipes, and add recipe ingredients to their lists.
Users create a unique account and are able to save their favorite recipes.
